The opening crawl is the signature device film of the Star Wars series, an American epic space opera franchise created by George Lucas. It opens with the static blue text, "A long time ago in a galaxy far, far away....", followed by the Star Wars logo and the crawl text, which describes the backstory and context of the film. composed by John Williams.

This sequence has featured at the main Star Wars saga theatrical films produced by Lucasfilm. Although it has retained the same basic elements, the opening crawl has significantly evolved throughout the series. It is one of the most franchise and has been frequently parodied.

The opening crawl was not used in the Star Wars "Anthology" films, "A long time ago in a galaxy far, far away....". Additionally, the opening sequence of Solo continues with the same style as the opening text.
